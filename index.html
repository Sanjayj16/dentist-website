Exams & Cleanings


Thorough dental exams and cleanings to maintain optimal oral health. Our skilled team performs comprehensive evaluations, checking for decay, gum disease, and oral abnormalities. Professional cleanings remove plaque and tartar, leaving your teeth fresh and polished.



03.

Cosmetic Dentistry


Transform your smile with cosmetic dentistry services. We offer teeth whitening, veneers, dental bonding, and smile makeovers. Enhance your confidence with a radiant, harmonious smile that reflects your unique personality and beauty.



04.

Gum Disease


Effective treatment for gum disease to restore oral health. Our experienced periodontists offer deep cleanings, scaling and root planing, and gum therapies to combat gum inflammation and infection Protect your gums and prevent further damage for a healthier, stronger smile.



05.

Painless Root Canals


Painless Root Canals Specialized root canal therapy to save infected teeth. Our skilled endodontists carefully remove infected pulp, clean the root canal, and seal it to prevent further infection. Relieve pain and preserve your natural tooth structure, promoting long-term oral health and functionality.



06.

Extractions


Painless tooth extractions for optimal oral health. Swift, gentle procedure with post-extraction guidance for a smooth recovery.



07.

Dental Crowns


Are you in need of a dental cap but unsure about the different types and their associated costs? Choosing the right dental cap is crucial for your oral health and aesthetics. At Smile n Shine Dental Clinic, our experienced dentists not only guide you in selecting the most suitable cap but also ensure that it’s a wise and lasting investment in your smile.



08.

Braces & Implants​


Transform your smile with braces and dental implants. Braces straighten misaligned teeth, while implants replace missing teeth with natural-looking results. Enhance your dental aesthetics and improve functionality for a confident, healthy smile.
